Grilled Cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Put 4 slices of cheese on bread
  3. Place bread in frying pan and fry one side
  4. Flip sandwich and fry other side
  5. Remove sandwich
